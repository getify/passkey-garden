Try out passkeys here

Unfortunately, it seems your browser does not support passkeys.

This page allows you to try out "registration" and "login" actions with passkeys, as if you were on a site/app that actually supports such options.

However, we DO NOT save or store your information, in anyway -- not in this browser, nor on our server. Zero, seriously.

Cleanup Old Passkeys?

If you register one or more passkeys here, the only thing leftover once you're done is that your device (or wallet/password manager) will have those passkey entries stored.

You may want to delete those entries after you're done here, as they're just clutter and will never do anything useful for this site (or anywhere else, obviously).

But that's entirely up to you!
